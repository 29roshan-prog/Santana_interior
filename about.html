About Santana Interiors

We offer solutions for your home needs in terms of designing and execution of the proposed plan for your space — be it bedroom, living, or kitchen. Our team of expert designers and carpenters bring nearly 15 years of industry experience.

No hourly charges — only based on square feet and kitchen units. We ensure complete satisfaction and our support continues even after project completion.

After the initial discussion at your site, we do a complete case study and send a proposal with full details — quotes, requirements, materials, finishes, accessories, and images.
